Amaravathi Museum

Best time to visit:-End of November and continues till the middle of February.
Timings:-10:00 am - 05:00 pm
Entry Fee:Just a meagre amount of Rs. 2 is collected as entry fee for adults, while there is no fee for kids under 15 years of age.
Famous For:-This Museum discloses the sculptures in the form of galaxy of and the images showcase content that were once a part of the Giant Stupa.
